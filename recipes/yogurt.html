Yogurt with granola and berries

Ingredients:

  • Full fat greek yogurt
  • Granola
  • (frozen) blueberries
  • Cinnamon
  • Maple syrypu (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. Defrost the blueberries, either the night before or in the microwave.
    2. Add the yogurt, blueberries, cinnamon and granola to a bowl.
    3. Drizzle maple syrup on top to taste.
