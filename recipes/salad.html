Salad with pears and walnuts

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Carrot
  • Ripe, juicy pear
  • Walnuts
  • Vinegar or lemon juice
  • Honey
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Julienne the carrot, or slice very thinly.
  2. Dice the pears and chop the walnuts.
  3. Add the mixed greens, carrot, pears and the walnuts to a bowl, and mix thoroughly.
  4. Make a dressing by mixing 1 part vinegar to 3 parts olive oil, and add honey to taste.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving.
