Butternut squash soup

Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Butternut squash
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • Bay leaf
  • Paprika
  • Nugmeg

Instructions:

  1. Peel and dice the onions, squash, carrots and potatoes. In a large pot, sautee the onions until soft. Add the squash and carrot and sautee a few more minutes.
  2. Add the potatoes, stock and bay leaf. Boil the soup for 30 minutes, or until the vegetables are very soft.
  3. Mix the soup with a (stick) blender. Add the paprika, nutmeg, and season to taste.
